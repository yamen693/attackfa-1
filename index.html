About Us

Attack Football Academy works to provide positive basics to players with high-quality techniques in an environment conducive to this. All training periods are specifically designed to work on the players’ personalities and develop their personal and physical skills. The academy works in training units in the physical, technical, psychological and tactical aspects. Participation of the two distinguished players in EFA League, Cairo region.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading football academy that nurtures young talents and prepares them for professional careers in football.

We aim to create a positive and inclusive environment where players can develop their skills, character, and love for the game.

Mission

Our mission is to provide high-quality training programs that focus on the physical, technical, psychological, and tactical development of our players.

We strive to instill values of teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship in our players, preparing them for success both on and off the field.
